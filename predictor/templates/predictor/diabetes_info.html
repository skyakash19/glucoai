What is Diabetes?

Diabetes is a chronic condition where the body is unable to properly regulate blood sugar (glucose) levels. It occurs when:

  • Insulin production is insufficient (Type 1)
  • The body doesn’t respond to insulin (Type 2)

Common Symptoms

  • Increased thirst and urination
  • Fatigue
  • Blurred vision
  • Slow wound healing

Healthy Habits to Prevent Diabetes

  • Maintain a healthy weight
  • Exercise regularly
  • Eat fiber-rich, low-sugar foods
  • Get regular health checkups
